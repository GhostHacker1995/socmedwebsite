The Need

Where Access Meets Action

CareConnect Amuru brings healthcare closer to the rural communities of Amuru by empowering Village Health Team members with the tools to reach every household.

In rural Amuru District, where up to 75% of diseases are preventable, over 900 Village Health Team volunteers serve as the first link between communities and the health system. Yet limited mobility — often requiring them to walk over 5km daily — restricts home visits and delays timely referrals.

Project Vision

"Efficient, equitable health services
for all in Amuru."

What We Do

Community Health Access

Repairing Broken Bicycles

We restore idle and broken bicycles, extending their lifespan and maximising existing resources.

Repairs are conducted at a VHT-managed mechanical workshop, equipped with essential tools and supported by trained VHT mechanics.

Expanding Bicycle Access

To broaden coverage and reach every VHT, we procure and distribute additional bicycles.

Locally sourced bicycles and international partnerships ensure cost-effectiveness and environmental responsibility.

Restoring the Mechanical Workshop

We are revitalising the Amuru VHT mechanical workshop as a hub for maintenance and sustainability.

The workshop operates with a community-supported maintenance model, building local expertise.

Driving Innovation: Bamboo Bikes

Piloting bamboo bicycle frames using renewable, locally available materials — an eco-friendly alternative.

Through partnerships, local artisans will be trained in bamboo frame fabrication, building a locally owned solution.
