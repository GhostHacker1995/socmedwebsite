OUR WORK IN ACTION

Our Impact: Transforming
Health Equity Across Uganda

From grassroots community support to national advocacy — SocMed Global Uganda is creating measurable change. Through partnership, education, and direct action, we confront structural inequities and build pathways to lasting health justice.

Challenging Structural Inequities in Health

Through global South coalition building, partnerships, and interdisciplinary organizing, we connect local lived experiences to global advocacy efforts aimed at advancing justice and dismantling systemic inequities in health and governance. Our work amplifies community voices in policy spaces.
READ MORE

Empowering a Conscious Generation of Young Leaders

Hosting student-led debates that bring together young voices to question, challenge, and unpack structural inequities, strengthening awareness and sparking meaningful conversations on justice and health equity. Over 300 students engaged across 5 universities.
READ MORE

Supporting Community Health Systems in Underserved Regions

We are helping frontline health workers reach households more quickly, improve referrals, and expand timely care to underserved communities in Amuru by equipping VHTs with bicycles and strengthening local maintenance systems. Result: 40% faster response to maternal emergencies.
READ MORE

Training the Next Generation of Equity-Focused Health Professionals

Annually, we support and deliver the Social Medicine Course as the Ugandan site alongside the other sites in Haiti and US, in partnership with EqualHealth. Over 120 clinicians, students, and community health workers trained in structural competency and accompaniment.
READ MORE

Care in the Midst of Crisis

When COVID-19 disrupted communities, in collaboration with EqualHealth, we supported frontline health workers in Amuru and Paimol communities with essential protective supplies — including masks, gloves, sanitizers, and risk communication materials, reaching 12 health facilities.
READ MORE

Answering the Call in Karamoja

Together with our partners, we reached hundreds of households in Abim District, Karamoja region with life-sustaining food support, offering relief in a moment of urgent need during drought and food scarcity. Provided emergency food packages to over 450 families.
