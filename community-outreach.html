COMMUNITY OUTREACH

Strengthening Community Health Systems for Equitable Care

We work to build resilient, community-driven health systems that ensure access to timely, equitable and quality care for underserved populations.

We believe that healthcare systems are only as strong as the communities they serve. Our Community Outreach programme works to strengthen these foundations — removing barriers, building local capacity, and supporting sustainable solutions that enable equity to reach every household.

Programme Vision

Equitable, community-driven health access for all.

Our Projects Support Us
THE CHALLENGE

Why It Matters

In many underserved settings, access to healthcare is affected by the interplay of several systemic challenges — limited infrastructure, workforce constraints, unfavourable climatic factors, limited education opportunities, and barriers such as distance and cost.

While they are often overstretched, under-resourced and unrecognized, community health systems play a critical role in bridging the gaps to health access. Strengthening these systems is essential to improving health outcomes, reducing preventable illness, and advancing health equity.

"We take a systems-driven, community-centered approach — combining practical solutions with long-term capacity building, strong partnerships, and continuous learning."

WHAT WE FOCUS ON

Our Focus Areas

By addressing both immediate barriers and underlying structural challenges, we create sustainable pathways to equitable healthcare access for all communities.
Contact Us
Community Health Service Strengthening

We strengthen community-based health service delivery by improving outreach systems, expanding coverage, and supporting frontline health workers to deliver timely and effective care.

Addressing Social Determinants of Health

We design and implement interventions that tackle the underlying barriers to health at household and community levels, ensuring access to care is not limited by social, economic, or environmental conditions.

Capacity & Workforce Development

We build the skills and competencies of community health actors, equipping them with the knowledge and tools needed to deliver quality services and respond to evolving health needs.

Governance & Financing

We support the strengthening of local health structures by improving governance systems and promoting sustainable financing mechanisms that enhance accountability and long-term functionality.

Evidence Generation & Learning

We generate and use data to understand what works, continuously improving our programmes and contributing to broader learning on community health and outreach effectiveness.

Partnerships & Ecosystem Strengthening

We work collaboratively with district and national stakeholders, as well as partners across NGOs, academia, and the private sector to expand reach, strengthen systems, and drive collective impact.

Sustainability & Resource Mobilization

We are committed to building sustainable models by diversifying funding, strengthening local ownership, and integrating successful approaches into existing community and health systems.

FEATURED PROJECT

CareConnect Amuru

CareConnect Amuru addresses transportation barriers to health access by equipping Community Health Workers (Village Health Teams) in Amuru District with bicycles and strengthening community-based systems for sustainable impact.

By removing the barrier of distance, we are helping frontline health workers reach households more quickly, improve referrals, and expand timely care to communities that need it most.
